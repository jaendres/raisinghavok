Who Rides With Us

Shiny and chrome. Also glued and primed.

Raising Havok is a miniature wargaming club for people who like their tabletops grim, their dice mean, and their paint jobs at least three thin coats deep. We push plastic across battlefields, talk trash across the table, and occasionally remember the actual rules. New blood always welcome — whether you're a tournament veteran or you just cracked your first starter box, there's a seat at the table and someone who'll teach you the game.

A Raising Havok Production

Mad Ork Lands

Our own web game: an orky vehicular battle royale, loosely bolted together from Gaslands-style rules. Build a rig in da Mek Shop — 50 teef, spend 'em on hulls, dakka, and dubious upgrades — then roll out into the Scrap Storm against the rest of the club. Last rig rollin' wins.

  • 7 hulls from Warbike to War Rig
  • Shootas, rokkits, skorchas, mines, oil slicks & a wreckin' ball
  • Multiplayer arenas with bot reinforcements
  • Club accounts — wins, kills an' damage tracked forever
